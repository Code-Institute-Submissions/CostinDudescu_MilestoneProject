About

The services that I offer, architecture, design, building and refurbishing houses, are based on hands on experience over a period of 18 years and following the three principles stated by Marcus Vitruvius Pollio, a Roman engineer, architect and artist, that architecture should accomplish:

Firmatis: It should stand up robustly and remain in good condition — be durable.
Utilitas: It should be useful and function well for the people using it — be functional.
Venustatis: It should delight people and raise their spirits — be beautiful.
